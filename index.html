About Us

Nirvana Homestay is a quaint little place for travellers who are looking for a comfortable stay away from the bustle of the main market/city area yet walkable distance to main points for eating and sights. Great for small groups of family and friends traveling together. We offer private rooms and a shared dormitory style accommodation. Small dinning area and a sit out.

We are conveniently located 10 minutes (walk) from the main bus stand, taxi stand and main market area popularly town as Old Super Market. You can be dropped off at the road right on the Kutchery Road to reach us. We believe in delivering safe, hygienic and convenient atmosphere to our guests.

Rooms

Family Room

Comes with attached bath, large kitchenette and an open terrace at the front. Ample space for a family of 4/5 and can easily accommodate groups of friends traveling together. The room is simple, clean, open and ample light. Room is carpeted and heater (during cold months) can be provided on extra cost.

Superior Room

We have 2 Rooms of this type. This room comes with a small kitchenette, attached bath and open terrace at the front. Space is enough for a group of friends 3/4 numbers and a family of 4 can fit easily. A great budget choice if you are looking to stay somewhere safe, clean and closer to main spots in Darjeeling town. Room is carpeted and heater (during cold months) can be provided on extra cost.

Dormitory Room

If you are a large group of friends or family then we offer great choice for a dormitory accommodation at Nirvana Homestay. Probably the only homestay in Darjeeling with a shared dormitory. 6 travellers can fit in very comfortable with ample space to move around. A small sit out offers a great opportunity for warm conversations. Please note that this is a shared space and you may be sharing the space with other strangers and travellers. Dormitory comes with s shared bathroom/toilet located outside the main sleeping area.

Kitchen and Dinning:

Our kitchen is placed at the lower floor of the building where we can cook and serve snacks and wholesome meals that should be ordered in advance. We are not a full fledged restaurant therefore the choice of food will be limited to home cooked meals that are wholesome and tasty. We serve both Indian and Continental breakfast however age menu will be limited.

Dining space is right where the lobby is, you can expect and enjoy homely atmosphere and healthy dinning experience.


Taxis & Sightseeing Arrangements:

Taxis in and around can be arranged though the homestay in very competitive rates. Entire sightseeing of Darjeeling can be easily and conveniently arranged through our staff at the homestay. If you like to pre-arrange this then call us on +91-906-482-4315.


Bookings:

Advance bookings can be done though our website and contact form. A small advance deposit will confirm your booking and ensure convenient and quick check in.

Our Dormitory is shared and on first come first serve basis unless your bed is booked in advance.
